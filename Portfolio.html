Case Study 01  ·  Client Advisory
Asset Allocation Review — Rising Rate Environment
Scenario
A hypothetical UK-based family office with £8m in a 60/40 portfolio sought guidance as central banks began aggressive rate-hiking cycles. Significant duration exposure in government bonds raised mark-to-market risk.
Analysis
Modelled the duration impact of a 200bps rate rise via Bloomberg — projecting a −14% MTM loss on the bond allocation. Compared reallocation scenarios into floating-rate instruments, short-duration credit, and private credit for yield pickup vs. liquidity trade-offs.
Recommendation
Phased rotation: reduce duration from 7 to 3.5 years by moving 30% of bonds into senior secured leveraged loans (SOFR + 400bps). Delivered as a 1-page investment rationale and 5-slide client summary.
